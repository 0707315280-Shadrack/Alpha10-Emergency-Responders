Our Services

Alpha 10 Emergency Responders provides professional, coordinated emergency and training services designed to safeguard lives and strengthen community resilience in Kenya and beyond.

Emergency Medical Response

Rapid response to medical emergencies including trauma, medical collapse, road traffic accidents and community incidents. Our certified responders stabilise patients and coordinate safe transfer to health facilities.

Disaster & Incident Management

Structured response to disasters and large-scale incidents using an Incident Command System. We support scene management, triage, evacuation and coordination with other agencies.

Event Medical Cover

Professional standby medical teams for public gatherings, sports events, corporate functions and community activities, ensuring immediate support in case of emergencies.

Training & Capacity Building

Structured training programmes including CPR, First Aid, basic life support and disaster preparedness for individuals, organisations and community groups.

Community Outreach & Safety Awareness

Health talks, safety campaigns and outreach initiatives aimed at empowering communities to prevent emergencies and respond calmly and effectively when they occur.

Partnerships & Consultancy

Collaboration with institutions, NGOs and private sector partners, including Pimac International, to design emergency response plans, drills and resilience programmes.
